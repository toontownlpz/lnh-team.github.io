Discover DSpico

DSpico is a project by the LNH team, consisting of two parts: an open-source DS(i) flashcart and an app launcher compatible with many existing DS(i) flashcarts.

All components —PCB, software, shell, stickers, and box art— are open-source, allowing you to build it yourself, modify it, study it or improve it.
